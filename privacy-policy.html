Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

LearnHealth Hub is a clinical microlearning app for primary care providers. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is stored, and your rights.

Data We Collect

LearnHealth Hub does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app runs entirely in your browser or as an installed application.

Local Storage

The app uses your browser's local storage to save:

  • Quiz progress (questions answered, correct/incorrect)
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Spaced review schedule
  • Game mode scores
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server, analytics service, or third party. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Analytics and Tracking

LearnHealth Hub does not use any analytics, tracking, cookies, or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on the GitHub repository.
